# Starfarer
A roguelike resource management game about space and the extinction of humanity.

*April 6th, 2032. Humanity is a lost cause. A virus broke out, and humanity's expansion efforts are only speeding it up. It's only a matter of weeks until everyone's dead. You throw on your suit, and hop in an engineering rocket, with 19 of the other on-board engineers. You look behind you... and you decide it's best to not tell the crew what you saw. With your little resources and your scattered research folders, you launch. Time for a new way to live... a new life.*

Main Gameplay Mechanics:
Asteroids - Bounce from asteroid to asteroid getting resources, on your main journey to live on the asteroid Kelt-32.
Management - Manage your resources, repair the hull, and shoot space rocks to make the journey safe.
Upgrades - Get better tools, better supplies, and more engineers, all to make your job slightly easier.
